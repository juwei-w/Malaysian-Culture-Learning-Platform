By offering traditional handcraft courses,
we are hoping to prevent the extinction
of making malaysian traditional products
and encourage youngsters to be the new
advocates of our beautiful cultures.

In this era, We see the flood of modern
minimalistic aesthetics are drowning the
other aspect of beauty in every industry.
The cultural influence has bring a low tide
of Malaysia culture products these days.
Nonetheless, We are inspired to see more
hand-make authentic delicacies and traditional
handcrafts from our own country, and extend
beyond here. Kita anak Malaysia!
